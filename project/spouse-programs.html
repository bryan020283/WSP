Couples Programs

Truffles and Trifles has excellent programs for couples of all types. Whether you’ve been married for 25 years and are looking for a creative way to spend your anniversary, you’ve been dating someone for months, or you’re taking a date out for the first time, cooking together is a fun, and unique way to impress and entertain your partner.

For the dating crowd out there, we have the way for you to stand out in the crowd. One thing many people admire in a date is their ability to be original and creative. How many dinner and movie combinations have they probably been on before you intrigue their interest with a fun and exciting cooking class? You will likely earn great points by surprising them with this option. Plus, there’s a bonus in it for you. If you end up staying with the person you’re dating, now they know how to cook!

Or if you’ve been with someone for a longer amount of time, this is a great way to build communication, have a great time together, spice up the relationship, and reignite the spark!

Cooking together in class is also a great idea for anniversaries of all kinds.

Team Building

Another great option for cooking classes is team building. Companies and office groups of all kinds use various team building techniques. Some of the many businesses who have used Truffles and Trifles since we began 27 years ago are: American Express, Bristol Myers, Johnson & Johnson, Hershey, Chep, Siemens, Lockheed-Martin, Cargill, Ritz Carlton, Raymond James, Cadbury, Exxon Mobile, Disney, Universal, Fidelity, Zero Chaos, State Farm, Marriott, Starwood, Pulte Homes, Novaris, Wyndam Hotels, Charles Schwab, The Orlando Sentinel, SunTrust, IBM, Chase Banks, Florida Gas Transmissions, and more law firms than we can mention.

Whether you want to come for the cooking alone, or have a meeting then get cooking, it’s entirely up to you. Surprise your work force with a fun and exciting team building experience that they will be sure to talk about for some time to come.

We have a large, state of the art, kitchen facility with teaching kitchens consisting of 14 cooktops, 19 ovens, 8 refrigerators, 3 freezers, a commercial deep fryer and 2 indoor gas grills, as well as 4 large grills used outside. We have 2500 square feet in one kitchen and 900 in our commercial teaching kitchen. So there is ample space for everyone in your group to get a hands on experience.
