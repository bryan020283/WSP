Couples Programs

Truffles and Trifles has excellent programs for couples of all types. Whether you’ve been married for 25 years and are looking for a creative way to spend your anniversary, you’ve been dating someone for months, or you’re taking a date out for the first time, cooking together is a fun, and unique way to impress and entertain your partner.

For the dating crowd out there, we have the way for you to stand out in the crowd. One thing many people admire in a date is their ability to be original and creative. How many dinner and movie combinations have they probably been on before you intrigue their interest with a fun and exciting cooking class? You will likely earn great points by surprising them with this option. Plus, there’s a bonus in it for you. If you end up staying with the person you’re dating, now they know how to cook!

Or if you’ve been with someone for a longer amount of time, this is a great way to build communication, have a great time together, spice up the relationship, and reignite the spark!

Cooking together in class is also a great idea for anniversaries of all kinds.
